# Security Review Checklist

Use this checklist before delivering results.

## Authorization and Scope

- authorization is explicit or the task is clearly defensive and local
- in-scope assets are stated
- out-of-scope assets or actions are stated
- safety constraints are preserved

## Discovery and Context

- target surface is identified correctly
- trust boundaries and sensitive flows are named
- major entry points or assets are listed
- relevant standards or compliance mappings are chosen deliberately

## Findings Quality

- each finding has evidence
- each finding distinguishes weakness from risk
- severity is justified and not inflated
- remediation is actionable
- uncertain items are labeled as gaps or follow-ups, not verified findings

## Coverage Quality

- the most relevant review themes were considered
- skipped areas are called out explicitly
- blind spots from missing access or artifacts are documented

## Delivery Quality

- summary is understandable by the intended audience
- report structure is consistent
- remediation priorities are clear
- residual risk and limitations are included
